  Some definitions, $\cos(x)$ and \(\sin(x)\) are
  \begin{equation}
    \label{eq:fefwe}
    \cos(x) \doteq \frac{e^{ix}+e^{-ix}}{2} \quad,\quad     \sin(x) \doteq \frac{e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2i}
  \end{equation}
